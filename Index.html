About Dr. Kiruba

She understands the female body from the inside out.

MBBS DNB (ObGyn) OB/GYN Specialist Clinical Nutrition Madurai Telemedicine Certified

Dr. Kiruba Lakshmi is a practicing Obstetrician and Gynaecologist in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, holding an MBBS and a DNB in Obstetrics & Gynaecology. After years of delivering babies and treating women's reproductive health, she saw a consistent pattern: most of her patients' health struggles were inseparable from how they ate.

She built NUTRIWAY Consultants to close that gap — a clinical nutrition practice where every recommendation is grounded in real medical understanding of the female endocrine system, reproductive biology, and the rich nutritional traditions of South Indian cooking.

She doesn't prescribe salads. She prescribes understanding.
